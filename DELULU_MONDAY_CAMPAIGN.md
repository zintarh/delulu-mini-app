## 🤔 What is Delulu?

**Delulu** is a decentralized prediction market platform built on Celo that lets you turn your boldest predictions into on-chain events. Here's what makes it special:

• **Create Delusions**: Post a public, verifiable claim about a future outcome
• **Stake Conviction**: Back your prediction with cryptocurrency (G$)
• **Community Voting**: Others can choose to "Believe" or "Doubt" your Delusion
• **Dynamic Pricing**: Watch your prediction's value fluctuate based on community sentiment
• **Win Rewards**: Successful predictions earn you both profit and social validation

Delulu combines **personal ambition, community consensus, and on-chain verification** to create a transparent prediction market powered by real conviction.

## 🏆 Climbing the Leaderboard

Your ranking is determined by three key metrics:

**1. G$ Staked 💵**
• The total amount of G$ (Celo Gold) you've staked across all your Delulus
• Higher stakes = higher conviction = better leaderboard position
• More exposure to potential rewards

**2. Unique Buyer Addresses 👥**
• The number of unique community members who've staked on your Delulus
• Shows how many people believe in your predictions
• Broader community engagement ranks higher
• Network effects matter!

**3. Trade Count 📊**
• The total number of trading interactions (Believe/Doubt votes) your Delulus have received
• Higher activity = more community interest
• Dynamic Delulus with viral potential rank higher

**Leaderboard Algorithm**: These three factors are combined to calculate your overall rank. Focus on creating compelling Delusions that attract community participation!
